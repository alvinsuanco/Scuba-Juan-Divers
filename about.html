About our Company

We are PROFESSIONAL SCUBA DIVING INTERNATIONAL (PSI) – Juan Divers. We are a group of divers and diving instructors duly recognized by the Worldwide Underwater Federation (CMAS) and the National Association of Underwater Instructors (NAUI). Our origins is the beautiful Brazil. Our rediscovery of the beauty of the Philippines’ 7,107 captivating islands and its magnificent seas has brought us halfway around the world to infect people with that same zest for diving and underwater exploration.

Mission

This Group inteds to introduce SCUBA Diving to the Filipino community in a way that is affordable, practical, fun and enjoyable while promoting and giving awareness to the importance of conservation and protection of marine life and its oceans.

Juan Divers aims to teach SCUBA diving in a way that is affordable and enjoyable while increasing awareness to the importance of conservation of our oceans.

WE LOVE NATURE!

The earth is too beautiful to waste, with the Philippines as the global epicentre of marine biodiversity. As a non-government organization that aims to protect and preserve the Philippines’ rich marine life through diving and exploration, we actively seek and keep an eye for unexplored diving sites. With this, we establish contacts with local authorities to help us implement advance protection methods on the sites and and rescue trainings to help local water rescue units more efficiently carry out rescue operations. Our main goal in each exploration is to help protect and preserve these God-given resources so that the next generations can experience what we are enjoying today.

WE BELIEVE IN THE DIVER IN EACH PERSON

We are fully equipped with the knowledge and the gears for deep diving rescue operations. We actively conduct rescue trainings with public and private agencies to raise awareness in caring for the seas. PSI is in partnership with the Philippine Coast Guard district, which enable us to respond to their requests for assistance whenever necessary.

We conduct affordable trainings and recreational underwater explorations for our beach patrons and visitors. From the moment they arrive at the beach to the time they surface the cool waters from a successful dive, we ensure that each experience is truly educational and highly enjoyable. This is the PSI commitment and our deep aspirations to create a diver in you.
