About Us

Juan Divers aims to teach SCUBA diving in a way that is affordable and enjoyable while increasing awareness to the importance of conservation of our oceans

If you are looking for some good advanture and meeting new friends above and below water, you are in the right place! Welcome to Juan Divers!

Mission

This group intends to introduce SCUBA Diving to the Filipino community in a wa tat is affordable, practical, fun and enjoyable while promoting and giving awareness to the importance of conservation and protection of marine life and its oceans.
